NeuroDrive

2021 UPS Hackathon

During my 2021 I.T. summer internship at UPS, I participated in the Hackathon as a member of the Data Devs team. Along with two data scientist interns and two web development interns, we built NeuroDrive, a framework combining machine learning models to detect unsafe driving behaviors based on sensor data and a user-friendly interface to display driver assessments. After placing in the top 4 of 24 teams, we were invited to present NeuroDrive to CEO Carol Tom√© and CIEO Juan Perez.

Flowchart of NeuroDrive framework from model to dashboard.

Model Development

For the machine learning models, both a random forest and BiLSTM were considered. We used publically available aggressive driving data consisting of signal information from accelerometers and gyroscopes to train and validate our models using Google Cloud Platform.
