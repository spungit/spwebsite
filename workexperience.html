Work Experience

Internships

  • Data Science Intern, United Parcel Service (June 2021 - August 2021)


    As an intern under Elizabeth Barayuga, I collaborated with data scientists in Europe to develop models for predicting actual weights and dimensions from manifest (user-input) quantities. I developed linear regression and multilayer perceptron (with embeddings) models for potential implementation. I also presented analyses of inbound and outbound package data from the Cologne (DE) hub, the largest European UPS hub, to the warehouse management and produced data and model visualizations with PowerBI.

    I also participated in the 2021 I.T. Hackathon where I collaborated with a team of data scientists and web developers over 48 hours to produce NeuroDrive, a framework using machine learning models to predict aggressive driving behaviors and display insights for management. We placed in the top 4 out of 24 teams and presented to UPS leadership, including the CEO and CIEO.

  • Graduate Research Intern, Los Alamos National Laboratory (June 2020 - May 2021)
